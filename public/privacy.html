Privacy Policy

Your data is protected with strong technical safeguards and strict access controls.

1. What We Collect

We collect only what is required to run the marketplace: your name, email, phone number, role (renter, landlord, agency), and any listings, messages or reviews you create on the platform.

2. Phone Number Masking

Phone numbers on public listings are never rendered in plain text on the page. Renters contact landlords through a masked WhatsApp deep-link that only reveals the number inside WhatsApp, protecting both parties from scraping and unwanted calls.

3. Encrypted Storage

All profile data is stored inside our encrypted Supabase database, guarded by Row-Level Security policies. Each user can only read and write their own private records; landlord and agency verifications are handled by trusted server-side functions.

4. What We Never Do

We never sell your data. We never share your contact details with advertisers. We never post on your behalf.

5. Cookies & Analytics

We use minimal first-party storage to remember your language choice and keep you signed in. We do not run third-party advertising trackers.

6. Your Rights

You can update your profile or request full account deletion at any time from your account settings, or by emailing support@smarthousefinder.co.tz.
