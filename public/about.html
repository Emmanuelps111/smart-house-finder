About Smart House Finder

We are a direct, commission-free housing marketplace that matches Tanzanian renters and students with verified local landlords — no agents, no hidden fees, no middlemen taking a cut of your rent.

Our mission

Finding a safe, affordable room in Dar es Salaam, Arusha, Mwanza or Dodoma should not require paying a broker a full month of rent up front. Smart House Finder puts renters in direct contact with the person who actually owns or manages the house — verified through our team — so every shilling of your budget goes toward your home, not to an intermediary.

Zero commission

We never charge renters, students or landlords a matching fee. Rent is paid directly to the landlord after you inspect the property.

Verified landlords

Every landlord and agency goes through identity checks before they can publish. Look for the blue verification badge on each listing.

Direct WhatsApp contact

Message landlords instantly through masked WhatsApp deep-links — no waiting for callbacks, no exposed personal phone numbers.
