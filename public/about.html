About Smart House Finder

We're on a mission to make finding student housing fair, transparent and stress-free — for renters and landlords alike.

Our story

Built by students, for students

Smart House Finder started in 2024 when a group of university students grew tired of broken listings, hidden fees and slow landlords. We built a platform that pairs trustworthy verification with a beautiful, modern experience.

Today we serve over 50,000 renters and 4,200 landlords across the UK.
