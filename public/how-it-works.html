How It Works

Two clear paths — one for renters and students, one for landlords and agencies.

1

Search verified listings

Browse the map or the list feed. Filter by region, neighbourhood, price and bedrooms to shortlist rooms near your campus or workplace.

2

Review the property page

Read the full description, view photos and video tours, check amenities and confirm the landlord verification badge before reaching out.

3

Contact via WhatsApp

Tap the WhatsApp button to message the landlord instantly. Your phone number is masked until you choose to share it.

4

Inspect in person, then pay

Always visit the property before paying anything. Payments happen directly between you and the landlord — Smart House Finder never collects rent or deposits.

1

Create your landlord account

Sign up as a landlord and complete your profile. Upgrade to an Agency account if you manage multiple properties.

2

Drop a map pin

From your dashboard, add a new listing and drop a precise map pin so renters can see exactly where the property sits.

3

Upload single or in bulk

Publish one property with photos and a short video, or use the Bulk CSV Upload (agency accounts) to import many listings at once.

4

Chat with renters on WhatsApp

Renters reach you directly through WhatsApp. No commission, no middlemen — you close the deal on your own terms.
